Point: <point> or <pnt>

Segment: <segment> or <segm>
Ray: <ray>
Middle Point: <midpoint> or <mid>
Bisection Line: <bisector> or <serper> or <srpr>
Parallel Line: <parallel> or <parl>
Perpendicular Line: <perpendicularline> or <perpline> or <perl>
Perpendicular Segment: <perpendicularsegment> or <perpsegm> or <pers>
Angle Bisector: <anglebisector> or <bisec> or <rat>
Intersection Point: <intersect> or <inter> or <int>
Hide object: <hide>
Show object: <show>
